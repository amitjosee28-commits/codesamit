More About Initiative

Voice of Students – महेन्द्र मा वि दार्चुला is a dynamic, student-driven initiative based at Mahendra Namuna Secondary School, created to ensure that every student's voice is heard, valued, and represented. It goes beyond a typical social media page by actively addressing real issues within the school—academic quality, management practices, student welfare, and transparency—while also celebrating achievements. The platform serves as a bridge between students and school authorities, encouraging open dialogue and responsible awareness.
